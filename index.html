About Pariwo

Pariwo is a safety technology company focusing on the latest safety-tech solutions to reduce crime and give peace of mind and equity in communities and cities at large.
Pariwo uses technology to prevent all forms of violence against women, girls, children and the vulnerable in Nigeria; In cases of intrusion or attack in the home; our technology will alert neighbours, friends, family and The Police of an impending/potential crime for rescue.

Our Objective

To work through Private and Public Partnerships (PPP) with our safety focused technology in vulnerable communities; deploying variety of community based safety solutions and services to tackle crime thereby creating flourishing SaferCommunities, and in turn breeds SaferCities in Nigeria.
We partner Corporate entities, NGOs and Government who support the deployment of our safety devices by distributing to female staff at their work places, students at schools as part of their Corporate Social Responsibility (CRS) to reduce crime and give confidence to the vulnerable in society.

Alarms provide 50 times more protection for your home than no security.
Take basic steps to prevent being targeted for GBV, Harassment, Rape, Burglary and other crimes by opportunist. We all want safer communities – with limited antisocial behaviour. That is why Pariwo SaferCities exists.

MAIGAARD Community Safety Alarm System

MaiGaard is a Home Security Alarm System that empowers homeowners and vulnerable communities with safety technology that links Neighbour to Neighbour, Neighbour to The Police via sms and calls; A Tap on our MaiGaard device to activate neighbor to neighbors, neighbour to Police Alerts when crime occur.

Pariwo Individual Safety Alarm

Pariwo Individual Safety Alarm is a security technology solution to reduce Gender Based Violence (GBV), bullying and reduce crime in Nigeria; On activation, the device releases a loud noise of 140db to deter attacker and attract attention. It helps vulnerable people including women walking alone, children, older adults at risk of medical emergencies and people who work at night.

Mini GPRS Personal Alarm System

The Mini GPRS Personal Alarm System is a real time tracking solution for families. It has an in built sms or email notification. It is designed for everyone including students, kids, people with special needs via our tracking platform. It can be geo-fence to create no go areas, inbuilt panic button for emergency and remote monitoring via our platform.

Safety Panic Application: SaferCity App

Safety Panic Application provides security and medical response that helps link victim to family, friends and the Nigerian emergency response network. It aids communication between Gender Based Violence victims and support network; social workers. This subscription based App can be downloaded from Google-Play Store.
