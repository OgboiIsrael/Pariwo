How Big is The Problem?

According to the World Health Organization, One in three women globally experience violence.

  • According to the 2018 National Demographic Health Survey (NDHS), 33% of women age 15-49 in Nigeria have experienced physical or sexual violence.
  • A recent United Nations Population Fund (UNPFA) Nigeria study found that 28% of Nigerian women aged 25-29 have experienced some form of physical violence since age 15.
  • The highest prevalence of physical or sexual intimate partner violence was reported in the African region, predominantly in sub-Saharan Africa.

About Pariwo


Pariwo is a safety technology company focusing on the latest safety-tech solutions to reduce crime and give peace of mind and equity in communities and cities at large.
Pariwo uses technology to prevent all forms of violence against women, girls, children and the vulnerable in Nigeria; In cases of intrusion or attack in the home; our technology will alert neighbours, friends, family and The Police of an impending/potential crime for quick rescue.
We collaborate Corporate entities, NGOs and Governments who support the deployment of our safety devices by distributing to female staff at their work places, students at schools as part of their Corporate Social Responsibility (CRS) to reduce crime and give confidence to women and other vulnerable people in the society.

MAIGAARD Community Safety Alarm System

MaiGaard is a Home Security Alarm System that empowers homeowners and vulnerable communities with safety technology that links Neighbour to All Neighbours, Neighbour to The Police via sms and calls; A Tap on a button on our MaiGaard device notifies a preconfigured rescue network of People, Street patrollers and The Police for quick rescue, response and crime detterence. With MaiGaard, you can call Emergency response units with a push of a button.
Features highlight:
  1. Built-in PIR and emergency buttons allowing for easy activation.
  2. The remote control provided, allows also for various functions, arm, disarm, panic, stay arm/silent panic.
  3. Internal 1800mah battery which allows up to 48hour operation should there be no power.
  4. Built-in motion sensor.
  5. Panic, Medical, Fire emergencies
  6. App Integration

Pariwo Individual Safety Alarm

Pariwo Individual Safety Alarm is a security technology solution to prevent Gender Based Violence (GBV), bullying and reduce crime in Nigeria; On activation, the device releases a loud noise of 140db to deter attacker and attract attention for your rescue. It helps vulnerable people including women walking alone, children, older adults at risk of medical emergencies and people who work at night.
Features highlight:
  1. Portable handheld device
  2. Easy activation by pulling handles.
  3. Produces a sound of 140 decibels to deter attacker and attract attention for rescue or other emergencies
  4. Internal battery - periodically replaced.
  5. Panic, Medical emergencies.

Mini GPRS Personal Alarm System

The Mini GPRS Personal Alarm System gives you a completely new level of awareness and convenience.
Loaded with GPS and WiFi positioning, It enables you to view real-time location from a computer, tablet or smartphone. Being stylish, tough and accurate with inbuilt panic button for emergency, it is ideal for Single women, Students, Children, the Elderly, and People with special needs. It can be geo-fenced to create no go areas.
Features highlight:
  1. Compact size.
  2. Panic button allowing for easy activation.
  3. GPS + LBS positioning.
  4. Audio Monitoring.
  5. Built-in battery with 2 Days of Battery Life.
  6. USB charger.

PariwoCam

The PariwoCam provides a simple but effective way to have a 24x7 view into your home. The compact, mobile and easily hidden video recording alarm system is small amd flexible enough to install anywhere in your home. A user friendly mobile application allows you to receive alerts on detection of humanoids, voices or sound, with live video feed available at all times for easy viewing. The PariwoCam also allows you to talk to perpetrators in your home and remotely activate a siren that will help scare off unwanted persons in your home.
Features highlight:
  1. Sound and motion detection.
  2. 2-way Audio.
  3. Night vision.
  4. Live video feed.
  5. Smart Phone App.
  6. Unplug alert.

Features highlight:
  1. Panic/emergency button for easy activation.
  2. Mobile application.
  3. Communication medium for GBV victims to support networks and Social workers.
