About Pariwo

Pariwo is a safety technology company focusing on the latest safety-tech solutions to reduce crime and give peace of mind and equity in communities and cities at large.
Pariwo uses technology to prevent all forms of violence against women, girls, children and the vulnerable in Nigeria; Even in cases of intrusion or attack in the home; our technology will alert neighbours, friends, family and The Police of an impending/potential crime for rescue.
At the tap of a button from a Pariwo device - neighbours, neighbouthood security and law enforcement agents are alerted and called to your rescue immediately.

To work through Private and Public Partnerships (PPP) with our safety focused technology in vulnerable communities; deploying variety of community based safety solutions and services to tackle crime thereby creating flourishing SaferCommunities, and in turn breeds SaferCities in Nigeria.
